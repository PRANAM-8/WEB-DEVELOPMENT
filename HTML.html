About Us

Founded in 1990 in Udupi, Karnataka, Tulasi has been serving people with authentic Ayurvedic care for over three decades. We are known for our commitment to quality, tradition, and personalized healing.

Our team uses the finest herbs and age-old techniques to ensure safe, effective, and natural remedies that bring harmony to body and mind.

Tulasi is not just a clinic, but a trusted center for holistic well-being and lifelong health.
